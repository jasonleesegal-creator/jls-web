We Turn Your Expertise Into a Show

01

Thesis & Story Strategy

Before a single cut is made, we ask what the episode is really about, not the guest's bio or the surface topic. That thesis becomes the spine every other decision gets built around.

02

Viewer-First Packaging

Packaging starts from the outcome the viewer wants, not what happened to be discussed. Every title and thumbnail direction answers one question honestly: why would a stranger click this?

03

Thumbnails Strategy

A thumbnail shouldn't just repeat the title. We pull the boldest claim from the episode and distill it into five words or less, a second layer of meaning that earns the click.

04

Coming Up Trailers

Every episode gets a 45 second trailer built like a mini-documentary, not a highlight reel: real tension, open loops, and unanswered questions that make someone think, "I need to watch this."

05

Full Episode Edit

We trim for pacing, not just length. Slow openings, repetition, and tangents get cut so the story of the show lands in the first five minutes, not buried under guest introductions.

06

Channel & Discovery Strategy

YouTube works like a search engine as much as a social feed. We build titles, descriptions, and channel structure so your best episodes keep finding new viewers months after they publish, not just in the first 48 hours.
